ABOUT US!



Glammee Beauty Salon & Spa is a low-cost and budget-friendly beauty salon that serves high-quality hair and nail services. Glammee Beauty Salon & Spa was established in March 2021 at Valenzuela St., Valenzuela City. This beauty salon and spa is a sole proprietorship kind of business and is solely owned by Ms. Peach Paulino-Ang. At the age of 21, she started her own business.

Glammee Beauty Salon & Spa was started by the love of the owner for nail arts and hair. Being inspired to also serve other people, she decided to build her own beauty salon and spa in the middle of the pandemic. At first, she started her service by offering home services because of the pandemic. Now that our community has started to be normal again, she opened her first physical store in Valenzuela City, and that Beauty Salon and Spa offers low-cost but high-quality services.
